➡️Sawal[1]:

ke Walid ka naam kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Huzoor khaja Moinuddin chisti radiallahu taala anhu
ke Walid ka naam khaja gayas uddin 
radiallahu taala anhu hai...

➡️Sawal[2]:
Hazrat khawaja  moinuddin chisti radiallahu taala anhu
ke walida ka.naam.kya ?
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat khawaja garib un  nawaz ke walida majida ka naam 
ummul wara aur bi bi mahe noor bhi kaha jata hai

➡️Sawal[3]:

ki wiladath khaan howi
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat khawaja moinuddin chisti radiallahu taala anhu
ki wiladath  saijistaan ke chote khasbe sijiz me howi 
saijistaan ko seestaan bhi kha jata hai

➡️Sawal[4]:

ki wiladath kab howi
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat khawaja garib nawaz radiallahu taala anhu
wiladath  14 rajjab ul murajjab  537 hijri ko howi

➡️Sawal[6];

➡️Sawal[7]:
Aap ka naam. moinuddin kis ne rhaka 
⭐Jawab:
Aap ka naam moinuddin aap ke waliden ne rhaka aur piyar  o 
shafaqhat se apne neak baqt bache ko hasan ke naam se pukarte 
the aur aaj bharat o pakistan  o bangladesh o digar mumalik 
ke musalman aqeedat o mohabat ke saat khawaja garib nawaz ke 
naam se aap ko yaad karte hai
