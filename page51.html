✨SEERAT E HUZOOR MUFTI E AZAM E HIND✨ 

   

➡️Sawal[1]:
Mufti azam e hind kiske shezade hain??
⭐Jawab:
Huzur Mufti azam e hind Allama wa moulana Mustafa raza khan 
Sarkar e ala hazrat imam e ahle sunnat sayyadi imam Ahmed 
raza khan fazile bareilvi alahi rehma ke dusre shabzade 
(bete)hain

➡️Sawal[2]:
Mufti azam e hind ki wiladat kis Tariq ko huvi ?
⭐Jawab:
Huzur sayyadi Mufti azam e hind Allama wa moulana Mustafa 
raza khan ki wila'dat e basa'dat 22zulhijja 1310 ko huvi

➡️Sawal[3]:
Mufti azam e hind ke peeer o murshid kon hai ??
⭐Jawab:
Huzur sayyadi Mufti azam e hind ke peeer o murshid Sayyed 
Abdul Hasan Ahmed noori alahe rehma hain

➡️Sawal[4]:
Mufti azam e hind ki wafat kab huvi?
⭐Jawab:
Huzur sayyadi Mufti azam e hind ki wafat 14 Muharram 
ul haram 1402 ko huvi !

➡️Sawal[5]:
Sibtain Raza Qadri kis ke pothe hain!?
⭐Jawab:
Huzur amin e shari'at sarkar ala hazrat ke bhai ustaad 
e zaman Allama Hasan raza khan ke pothe hain
(rehmatullahi ta'ala alai)
