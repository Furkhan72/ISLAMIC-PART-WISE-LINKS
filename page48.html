✨SEERAAT E AMEER E MUAWIYA RADIALLAHU TAALA ANHU✨ 

➡️Sawal[1]:
Hazrat ameer e muawiya kon hai 
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat  ameer e muawiya radi allah hu ta'ala anhu sahabi e 
rasool hai 
Har sahabi e nabi janati hain 
Aur kohi agar  ameer  e.muawiya  ki shaan me. Bhokne tho sunle 
Imam hasan ne ameer e muawiya  se sulha karli hum us muawiya  
se jang nahi kar sakte aur imam husain ne yazid se jang ki hum 
us yazid se khabi sulha nahi kar sakte shaer kehta hain
Kul sahaba taare hai ahele bayith aise hain  ameer e muawiya  
hamare hain!

➡️Sawal[2]:
Hazrat  e Ameer e muawiya ke walid ka naam kya hai 
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat e Ameer e muawiya Razi Allah tala anhu ke walid e 
muhtaram ka naam hazrat abu sufiyan razi Allah tala anhu hai

➡️Sawal[3]:

momineen hain 
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat e Ameer e muawiya Razi Allah tala anhu  ke bahen jo um
ul momineen hai hazrat ume habiba Razi Allah tala anha
