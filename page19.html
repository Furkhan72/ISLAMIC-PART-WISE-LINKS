✨MAKRUHAT KE MASAIL ✨ 


➡️SAWAL[1]:
Makru e tahrimi kise kahte Hain ?
⭐JAWAB:
Jis ke karne se ibadat naqis ho jati or karne 
wala gunahgar ho jata hai uska gunah haram se kam nahi 

➡️SAWAL[2]:
Makru e tanzihi kise kahte hai ?
⭐JAWAB:
Makruh e Tanzihi wo kam hai jo shariyat ko pasand nahi 
bachna sawab hai 

➡️SAWAL[3]:
Muh me koi cheez liye hue namaz padna kaisa hai ?
⭐JAWAB:
Muhh mein koi cheez liye hue namaz  padhana makrooh hai, 
jab ke Qir'at se Maney na ho aur agar Maney Qir'at ho, 
maslan aawaz hi na niklay ya is qisam ke alfaaz nikleen 
ke Qur'an ke na ho, to namaz fasid ho jaye gi....

⭐Jawab:
Makruh waqton mein quran shareef na padhe to behtar hai 
aur padhe to kohi harj nahi
