✨MUSAFIR KI NAMAAZ ✨ 

   MUSAFIR KE NAMAAZ

➡️Sawal[1]:
Shariyat me musfir kise kahete hai?
⭐Jawab:
Shariyat me musafir wo shaks hai jo teen roz ki raha jane 
ke irade se basti se baher howa

➡️Sawal[2]:
musafir per namaz ke bare me kya hukum hai? 
⭐Jawab:
Musafir safar me namaz ko  khasar karna chahiye  Yani chaar
rakat wali farz namaz ko do kar ke phadni chahiye 
Musafir ke haaq me do hi rakat puri namaz hai 

➡️Sawal[3]:
khasar kise kahte hai?
⭐Jawab:
Safar me char rakat wali farz namaz ko do kar ke phadne ko 
kasar kahete hai 

➡️Sawal[4]:
musafir par khasar karna kya hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Musafir par kasar karna wajib hai 

➡️Sawal[5]:
Kya fajar magrib witar ki khasar hai?
⭐Jawab:
Fajar magrib witar ki kasar nahi hai 
⭐ جواب 
فجر مغرب وتر کی قصر نہیں ہے

➡️Sawal[6]:
kya sunnaton ko bhi khasar karke phadna chahiye ?
⭐Jawab:
sunaton me kasar nahi hai 
Agar mauqa ho tu puri phadhe warana maaf hai 

➡️Sawal[7]: 
Agar kohi shaks teen din ki raha kam waqt me taye kar le 
tu musafir hoga ya nahi ?

⭐Jawab:
Agar kohi shaks motar gadi, rail gadi, hawai jahaz, wagira 
se  teen din ke  raha kam waqt me taue kar le tu musafir 
hojaye ga khawan kitni hi jaldi karle 

➡️Sawal[8]:
Musafir kis waqt se Namaz me khasar shuru kare ?
⭐Jawab:
Musafir jab basti ki aabadi se dur ho jaye tu us waqt se 
namaz me kasar shuru kare 

➡️Sawal[9]:
Teen roz ke raha ke irade se nikla magar ye bhi hai darmiyan 
me ek din taherongga tu musafir hoga ya nahi ?
⭐Jawab:
Agar teen din ki raha ke irade se nikla hai aur darmiyan 
me taherna zamani taur par hai tu musafir rahega Aur agar 
is irade se nikla hai ke hai do din ki raha jata hoon aur 
phir ek din ki raha par jaonga tu musafir nahi hoga

➡️Sawal[10]:
Is zamane ke hisab se teen roz ki miqdar kitni hai 
⭐Jawab:
Is zamane ke hisab se teen din ki miqdar 92 kilo miters hai 

➡️Sawal[11]:
Musafir Agar muqeem ke piche Namaz phade tu kya kare ?
⭐Jawab:
Musafir agar kisi muqeem ke piche phade tu puri phade qasar
na kare

➡️Sawal[12]:
Muqeem agar musafir ke piche namaz phade tu kya kare
⭐Jawab:
Muqeem agar musafir ke piche phade tu imam ke salam.pher 
dene ke bad apni baqi do rakaten phade aur in rakaton me 
qirat bilkul na kare balke sure fatiha phadne ki miqdar 
chup chap khade rahe 

➡️Sawal[13]:
Agar musafir imam ne qasar nahi kiya aur puri char rakat 
phada di tu muqtadi ki namaz howi ya nahi 
⭐Jawab:
Agar musafir imam ne qasar nahi kiya aur puri char rakat 
phada di tu muqtadi ki namaz nahi howi

➡️Sawal[14]:
Kisi musafir ne ke char rakat phad li tu kya us ki namaz howi?
⭐Jawab:
Agar kisi ne qasdan chaar phad li aur dusra qaida kiya tu farz 
aada ho gaya aur akhiri do  rakaten nafil  hogayi magar gunah 
ghaar aur mustahaq naara howa aur tauba kare
Aur agar do rakat par qaida na kiya tu farz aada na howa

➡️Sawal[15]:
Kya bustand aur railway station per qasar kare ga ya nahi? 
⭐Jawab:
Agar aabadi se baher ho aur teen din ki raha tak ka safar ka 
irada bhi ho tu bustand aur railway station par qasar karega 
warna nahi
