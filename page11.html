✨ ISTENJA KE MASAIL ✨ 

➡️Sawal[1]:
Istenja khane me jane se pahel bismilla phad na kisa hai 
⭐Jawab:
Istenja khane me jinnat aur shayateen rahete hai agar jane 
se pahele  bismilla phad li jaye tu is ki barakat se wo satar 
dhek nahi sakte Jin ki aahnkoo ke aur logon ki satar ke 
darmiyan parda rahega bahetar ye hai ke ye dua phade

➡️Sawal[2]:
Istenja khane me daqil hote waqt konsa qadam pahele rhakna 
chahiye? 
⭐Jawab:
Istenja khane me daqil hote  waqt pahele ultha qadam rhake 

➡️Sawal[3]:
Baitul kala me Jane se pahle sar ko dhaank na chahiye ya 
nahi ?
⭐Jawab:
Baitul kala me sar ko dhaank kar jana chahiye naange sar 
baitul kala me dakil hona mamnu hai

➡️Sawal[4]:
Istenja ke waqt  najasat ki jaga ko kab tak dhona chahiye?
⭐Jawab:
Achi tarha najasat ke maqam ko dhole is tarha dhoe ke chiknai
ka asar baqi na rahe

➡️Sawal[5]:
Bait ul kala se baher nikalte waqt konsa qadam pahele baher 
rhkna chahiye?
⭐Jawab:
Bait ul kala se baher nikalte waqt sida qadam pahle baher 
rakhna  chahiye
