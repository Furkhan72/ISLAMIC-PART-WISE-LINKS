➡️Sawal[7]:
Perfum laga kar namaz phad kaisa hai?
⭐Jawab:
Parfume laga kar namaz phadne se namaz nahi hogi
Kyon ke parfume me Alcohlo hota hai alcohol sharaab 
hai Aur sharab na paak hai Agar kapde pe ek dhiram se 
ziyada lag jaye tu is paak karna farz hai jisam ke jis
hise par lage use dhona zarori hai agar is haalat men 
agar namaz phade tu namaz hogi hi nahi

kataata ho Ayi se ki imamat khabil e qubool nahi aur in ke

koi imam nahi rhakta hai tu wo fasiq e mualin howa
