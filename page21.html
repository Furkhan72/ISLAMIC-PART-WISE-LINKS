me chulah jalane se mayyit ko Takleef hoti hai 
kya ye durst hai?
⭐JAWAB:  
Aksar log ye kahete hai ke mayat ke ghar teen din 
tak chulah nahi jalana chahiye is se mayyit ko Takleef
hoti hai ye khayal  durst nahi hai ye hamare gharon ke 
andar jahelana.gair sharai unhi me se hai Taleemi sharrah 
ye hai ke mayyit ke ghar pahele din Rishtedar  padosi 
wagair khana de.de.tu bahetar kyon ke un logon ke liye 
ye sab kArna ek Azmaish ho jati hai magar  chulah jalane 
se mayyit ko Takleef  hoti hai ye khayal durst nahi hai

➡️SAWAL[9]:
Namaz e janaza ke tareeqa kya hai?
⭐JAWAB:
JANAZE KI NAMAZ KA TARIKA

1)NIYAT : 
Niyat ki maine namaze janaza ki takbeer waaste Allah Ta'ala 
ke liye , Durood Huzur ﷺ k liya, dua is mayyat ke liye 
peeche is imam ke moonh mera taraf kaaba Shareef ke

2)TARIKA: 
Imam sahib Allahu Akbar kahete hue dono haatho ko kaano tak 
utha kar niyat baandh lenge hum bhi aisa hi karenge 

Phir sana pade jo hum namaz me padte hain, sana me wajaalla 
sanauka mazzed padhe

- Subhanaka allahumma bi humdika wata baara kasmuka watala 
jadduka wajalla sanauka walaiha gairuk

Phir imam dusri takbir padega hum bhi aahiste se padenge 
aur Durud e Ibrahim padenge jo namaz me padte hai

Phir imam teesri takbeer padenge hum bhi aahiste se 
padenge ab namaz e janaza ki dua padenge na yaad ho 
todua-e-maasoora padenge ye bhi na yaad ho to 
aksar ulema dua padte hai
(rab bana aataina fid duniya hasanatau wa fil 
aakhirati hasanatau wa kina azaa bannar) 
Agar ye bhi na yaad ho to sure fateha pade 
aur koshish kare ki janaze ki dua yaad ho jaye

Phir imam chauthi takbir padenge hum bhi aahiste 
se padenge

Aur takbir ke baad dono haath chod denge aur dahine 
aur bayein janib imaam k baad salam pherege

Yaad rahe is namaz me takbir me haath nahi uthana hai 
sirf paheli takbeer me haath uthaenge baaki kisi bhi 
takbir me haath nahi uthaenge
