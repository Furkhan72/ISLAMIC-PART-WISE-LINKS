✨AZAAN KE MASAIL ✨ 

➡️Sawal[1]:
Farz namaazo may azaan kehna kaisa hai?
⭐JAWAB:
➡Farz namaazo ko jamaat ke saat masjid me adaa karne ke 
liye azaan kehna sunnate muakkada hai, magar uss ka hukm 
misl waajib ke hai yaani agar azaan na kahi gayi toh waha 
ke sab loog gunehgaar honge.

➡️Sawal[2]:
Azaan kis waqt kaheni chahiye?
⭐JAWAB:
➡Jab namaaz ka waqt ho jaaye toh azaan kehni chahiye Waqt 
se pehle jaayiz nahi agar waqt se pehle kahi gayi toh waqt 
hone par lautayi jaaye

➡️Sawal[3]:
Iqamat ke waqt  logon ka khada rahena  kyisa hai?
⭐Jawab:
Iqamat ke waqt logon ka khada rahena makru wo.mana hai 
lehaza is waqt Baithe rahe phir jab takbir kahene wala 
hayya ala ll falah par phunche tu taher jaye 

➡️Sawal[4]:
Kya azaan kahene wala he iqamat kahen ya phir dusre bhi 
kahe sakta hai
⭐Jawab:
Haan  Azaan kahene wala he iqamat kahen  is ke  ijazat 
ke  bagir dusra na kahen agar bagir ijazat dusre ne kahi 
aur azaan kahene  wale.ko na gawara ho tu makru hai

➡️Sawal[5]:
Agar suniyaon ki masjid me koi wahabi bad mazhab Azan de 
tu kya azan phir se phadi ni hogi
⭐Jawab:
Agar suniyaon ki masjid me koi wahabi bad mazhab azan de 
tu azan phir se phadni hogi 

➡️Sawal[6]:
Agar koi bad mazhab Azan de tu phir se azan na phade tu 
namaz hogi ya nahi?
⭐Jawab:
Agar koi bad mazhab azan de tu phir se azan na phade tu 
namaz ho jaye gi lekin sunnat e mukada ka tark ho gaya 
kyon ke azan sunat e mukada hai agar koi bad mazhab azan 
de tu azan ko dubara de 

➡️Sawal[7]:
Azaan ka behtar tariqa kya hai ?
⭐JAWAB:
➡Masjid ke sahan se baahar kisi buland jagah par Qible 
ki taraf muh kar ke khade ho aur doono haatho ke kalimah 
ki ungliyo ko kaano me daalkar buland aawaaz se azaan ke 
kalimaat ko tehar tehar kar kahe jaldi na kare aur Hayya 
alassalaat kehte waqt dahine jaanib aur Hayya alalfalaah 
kehte waqt baaye jaanib muh phere.

➡️SAWAL[8]:
Azan me kitne farz hai aur wo kya hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Azan me 2 farz hai aur wo 
1) hayya alas salah 
2) hayya alal falah

➡️SAWAL[9]:
Azaan me kitne sunnate hai aur kya hai?
Azaan me (10) sunnatain hain :
1)  ba wuzu hona
2)  oonchai par khade hona
3)  Masjid mein dayen janib khada hona
4)  Dayen baen gardan ghumana
5)  Ungliya kaanon mein daalna
6)  Qible ki taraf munh hona
7)  Sahih Azaan kehna
8)  Saaray kalmaat azaan ke alag alag ada karna
9)  Ungliya kaanon mein hilaana aur
10) Bahar sehan mein khade rehna .
