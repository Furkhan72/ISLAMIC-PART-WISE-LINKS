✨ TAYAMMUM KE MASAIL ✨ 

➡️Sawal[1]:
Tayamum me kitni cheezen farz hai aur wo kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Tayamum me teen 3 cheezen farz hai 
(1)niyat karna 
(2)pure muh par haat paher na
(3)dono haaton ka koniyaan  samet masa karna 
Agar Anghooti paheni ho tu is ke niche haat pahere  
na farz  aur aurat agar zewar pahene ho tu is ko 
hata kar haat paher na farz hai

➡️Sawal[2]:
Kin cheezon se tayamum kar na jaez hai
⭐Jawab:
Paak.mitti  phatar reet multani mitti  kachi ya paki 
aent phatar ya chune ki diwar 
In cheezon se tayamum karna jaez hai 

➡️Sawal[3]:
Tayamum karna kab jaez hai 
⭐Jawab:
Pani nahi hone ki surat me Itni sardi ho ke pani ke 
istemal se mar jane ya bimaar hone ka andesha ho

➡️Sawal[4]:
Agar pani se bukhar badd ne ka Andesha ho tu tayamum 
kar sakte hain? 
⭐Jawab:
Agar pani se bimar i me izafa hone ka  andesha ho tu
tayamum karsakte hai 

➡️Sawal[5]:
Tayamum kaise karen?
⭐Jawab:
Tayammum ki niyat se Bismillah kehkar kisi aisi paak 
cheez par jo zameen ki Qism se ho.. Dono haath maar 
kar ulat le,  Agar ziyada Garad ( yanni dhool) lag 
gai ho  to haath jhaade. Aur us se saare mooh ka masah 
kare. Phir doosri martaba yunhi haath maare, aur nakhun 
se lekar kohni (elbow) samet ya dono hatho ka masah 
kare tayammum ho gaya.

➡️Sawal[6]:
Tayamum kab karna chahiye
⭐Jawab:
Pani par qudrat na tab tayamum karna chahiye 
Maslan :
Pani itni dur hai whaan phunch nahi sakte  Aur maraz 
Aisa hai ke pani se wazu karne se maraz bhad jaye Aur 
ek Aisi surat hai ke pani hai magar namaz ka  waqt 
khatam ho jaye ga Aisi surat me bhi tayamum kar sakte hai

➡️Sawal[7]:
Ye Guman hai Ke Wuzu Ya Gusl Karne Me Eid Ki Namaz 
Jaati Rahegi Khwa Yu Ke Imam Padh Kar Faarig Ho Jaega Ya
Zawaal Ka Waqt Aajaega, Dono Surto Me Tayammum karna kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Ye Guman hai Ke Wuzu Ya Gusl Karne Me Eid Ki Namaz Jaati 
Rahegi Khwa Yu Ke Imam Padh Kar Faarig Ho Jaega Ya Zawaal 
Ka Waqt Aajaega, Dono Surto Me Tayammum jayez hai.
