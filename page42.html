✨SEERAT E OMER E FAOOQ RADI ALLAHU TAALA ANHU✨ 

➡️SAWAL[1]:
Hazrat e omer e Farooq radiallahu taala anhu ku Farooq 
laqab kyun Mila ?
⭐JAWAB:
Hazrat e omer e Farooq radiallahu taala anhu ku Farooq 
laqab mila kyun ki wo bhoot sahi aur galat me fark karne 
wale hai 

➡️Sawal[2]:
Aise kon se sahabi hain jin ke islaam lane ke liye huzoor 
Nabi e Akram sallallahu alaihi wa sallam ne dua maangi hai?
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat e umr e Farooq radiallahu taala anhu  
sahabi hain jin ke islaam lane ke liye huzoor Nabi e Akram 
sallallahu alaihi wa sallam ne dua maangi hain.........

➡️SAWAL[3]:
wo kon se sahabi hai jin ki Nekiyaan Asaman ke sitaraon ke 
barabar hai 
⭐JAWAB:
Hazrat Umar e farooq Razi Allah Tala Anhu ki Nekiyaan Asmaan 
ke sitaraon ke barabar hai

anha Jo huzoor sallallahu alaihi wa sallam ke nikah 
me aayi hai..

Umar e faruq radi allahu ta'ala anhu ka hain
