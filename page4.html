✨ NAMAZ KE MASAIL ✨

➡️Sawal[1]:
Paint fold kar ke namaz phad na kya hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Paint fold kar ke namaz phad na makruh e taherimi hai 
Makruh e tahrimi se namaz  dubara phad na hoga 

➡️Sawal[2]:
Shirt in kar ke namaz phad na kya hai 
⭐Jawab:
Shirt in kar ke namaz phad na makruh e tahrimi hai 

➡️Sawal[3]:
Haalat e namaz ma Daman samate (fold) kar ke namaz padna 
Kaisa hai?
⭐Jawab:
Haalat e namaz me daman samate na fold kar ke namaz padhna 
makru e tahrimi  hai 

➡️Sawal[4]:
Jis kapde par jaandaar ki tasweer (photo) ho usko pehen kar
namaaz padhna kaisa hai?
⭐Jawab:
jis kapde par jaandaar ki tasweer (photo) ho usko pehen kar 
namaaz padhna makaru e tahreemi hai
 
➡️Sawal[5]:
Agar Shirt ya t-shirt half asteen ki ho aur us ku pahan kar 
namaaz padna kaisa hai?
⭐Jawab:
T shirt half asteen pahen kar namaz phadna.makruh e tanzihi 
hai yani namaz ho jaye gi lekin sawab me kami hogi

➡️Sawal[6]:
Jandar ki tasveer wale ghar me namaz hogi ya nahi? 
⭐Jawab:
Kisi maqluq ki tasveer ke aage namaz nahi hogi Agar dhaki 
chupi ho tu ho jaye gi Ghar me insano ki tasveer lagana 
haram hai Chote bachon ke kilone jayise godiyaan bhi ghare 
na rhake

⭐Jawab:
PHOTO LAGANA HARAM HAI...AUR NAMAZ NHI HOGI VAHAÑ...AUR NAMAZ 
PADHLI TO PHIR SE LAUTANI HOGI
