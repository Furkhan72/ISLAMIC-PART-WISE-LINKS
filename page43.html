✨SEERAT E HAZAT E OSMAN E GANI✨

> 

➡️Sawal[1]:
zun noorain kisko kaha jata hain???
⭐Jawab:
zunnoorain ameer ul mominen sayedna usman e ghani 
radi allahu ta'ala ann ko kaha jata hain

➡️Sawal[2]:
Hazrat e Usman e gani [R A] ku zun noorain laqb kyu Mila ?
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat e Usman e gani [R A] ku zun noorain laqb is liye Mila 
ke  unho ne Nabi e paak ki doo betiun se nikha Kiya hai .

➡️SAWAL[3]:
Hazrat e Osman e gani[R A] ka wisal kab hua hain?
⭐JAWAB:
Hazrat e Osman e gani[R A] ka wisal  18 zil hijja ku hua hai..

➡️SAWAL[4]:
Nam ni had Sunni rafzi aur shia loog Eid e ghadeer kis ke wisal
per manate hai?
⭐JAWAB: 
Nam ni had Sunni rafzi aur shia loog Eid e ghadeer , 
Hazrat e Osman e gani [R A] ke wisal per manate hai......

➡️SAWAL[4]:
Hazrat e Osman e gani [R A] ke wisal per shia aur rafzi ke
kon se Eid manate Hain?
⭐JAWAB:
Hazrat e Osman e gani [R A] ke wisal per shia aur rafzi ne 
Eid e ghadeer manate Hai....

➡️Sawal[5]:

nikah me aayi
Hazrat ruqayya binte mohammad ﷺ aur un ke wisal ke baad 
hazraat ume kulsum binte mohammad ﷺ

➡️Sawal[6]:

⭐Jawab:
Hazrat osman e ghani Razi allah tala anhu khalifa e suwam hain .

bin abu al aas
