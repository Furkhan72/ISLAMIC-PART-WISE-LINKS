✨SEERAT E HAZRAT E ALI RADIALLAHU TAALA ANHU✨

> 

➡️SAWAL[1]:
Hazrat e Ali radiallahu taala anhu ku saheed karne 
wala paleed o jahannami shakhs kon hai ?
⭐JAWAB:
Hazrat e Ali radiallahu taala anhu ku saheed karne 
wala paleed o jahannami shaks ABDUL REHMAN IBN E MULJIM hai.

➡️Sawal[2]:
Hazrat e Ali radiallahu taala anhu ka mashoor o zamana 
laqab kya hai jo un ku Nabi e paak ne deya tha ?
⭐Jawab:
Hazrat e Ali radiallahu taala anhu ka mashoor o zamana laqab  
ASADULLAH hai jo un ku Nabi e paak ne deya tha .......

➡️Sawal[3]:
Haidar e karrar sher e khuda kis ko.khete hai.  
⭐Jawab:
Haidar e karrar sher e khuda hazrat e ali razi allahu 
taala ann ko khete hain 

➡️Sawal[4]:
Hazrat fatim razi allha anha ki kitni auladen hai 
⭐Jawab:
Sayeda zaheda tayyiba tahera Fatima tuzzhar'a radi 
allahu ta'ala anhu ko theen betiyan hain 
1.Hazrate Ruqaiya 
2.Hazrate umme kulsum
2.Hazrate zainab (radi allahu ta'ala anha!)

➡️Sawal[5]:
Sayeda fatima tu zahra radi allahu ta'ala anha 
ko kitne shezade(son's) hain???
⭐Jawab:
Sayeda zaheda tayyiba tahera Fatima tuzzhar'a radi 
allahu ta'ala anhu ko 3 shezaden hain 
1.hazrate imam e Hasan radi allahu ta'ala anhu
2.hazrate imam e Hussain radi allahu ta'ala anhu
3.hazrate  mohsin radi allahu ta'ala anhu.
