        WITR KA BAYAN
 
➡️Sawal[1]:
Witr mein Qunoot ki takbir se pehle donon haathon ko  
kanoon tak  auuthana kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Witr mein Qunoot ki takbir se pehle donon haathon ko  
kanoon tak  auuthana sunnat hai...

➡️Sawal[2]:
Namaz e witr ka tariqha kya hai
➡ سوال
 نماز وتر کا طریقہ کیا ہے 
⭐Jawab:
Namaaze Witr Bhi Isi Tarah Padhi Jaati Hai Jis Tarah Aur 
Namaaz Padhi Jaati Hai. Lekin Witr Ki Teesri Rakat Mein 
Alhamdu Aur Surat Padhne Ke Baad Kaanon Tak Dono Haath Le 
Jaye Aur Allahuakbar Kehte Huwe Haath Waapas Laaye Aur Naaf 
(Navel) Ke Neeche Baandh Le Phir Dua-E-Qunoot Padhe Phir Us 
Ke Baad Aur Namaazon Ki Tarah Ruku Aur Sajda Wagairah Karke 
Salaam Pher De

➡️Sawal[3]:
Zaid ka suwal hai ki namaz e witr mein duae qunoot na padhe 
tho kya hukm hai?
⭐Jawab:
Agar Duae Qunoot Qasdan (Jaan Boojh Kar) Na Padhe To Namaaze 
Witr Phir Se Padhe Aur Agar Bhul Kar Na Padhe To Aakhir Mein 
Sajdae Sahw Kare. (Bhare Shariat)
