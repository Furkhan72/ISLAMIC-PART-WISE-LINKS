➡️Sawal[37]:
 kin namazon ki khaza zarori hai
⭐Jawab:
Farz ki qaza farz hai Witar ki qaza Waajib hai. Aur 
Fajr ki sunnath farz ke Saath Qaza ho Aur Zawaal se 
pahele Padhe to farz ke Saath sunnath bhi Padhe agar 
Zawaal ke baad Padhe to Sunnath ki qaza nahi. Aur agar 
Fajar ki farz padhli aur sunnath rahe gayi to 20 minute
din nikalna se pahele is ki qaza padhna gunaah hai. 
Aur Zuhor aur juma ke pahele ki Sunnatein Qaza Hogayin 
Aur Farz padhli Agar waqth qatam hogaya to un Sunnathon 
ki qaza nahi aur Agar waqth baaqi hai to padhe aur Afzal 
yeh hai ke pichli Sunnatein padhne ke baad inko padhein
