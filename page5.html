✨ AAYAT E SAJDA KA BAYAN ✨ 

➡️SAWAL[1]:
Agar Ayat-e-Sajdah sunne ki niyyat na thi, 
lekin bilakasur sun liya to sajda karna kya hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Agar Ayat-e-Sajdah sunne ki niyyat na thi, Sunne 
wale ke liye zaroori nahin hai bilakasur suni ho, 
bilakasur sunne se bhi Sajdah wajib ho jata hai.

➡️SAWAL[2]:
Agar Ayat-e-Sajdah ka tarjuma suna to is surat 
mein sajda karna kya hai?
⭐JAWAB: 
Har zaban mein Ayat ka tarjuma padhne aur sunne 
wale par Sajdah wajib ho jata hai, sunne wale 
ne samjha ho ya na samjha ho.

➡️SAWAL[3]: 
Agar namaz ke bahar Ayat-e-Sajdah parhi to kya 
fauran Sajdah kar lena wajib hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Ayat-e-Sajdah namaz ke bahar parhi to fauran Sajdah 
kar lena wajib nahin, lekin wuzu ho to ta'kheer( late)  
karna makrooh-e-tanzeehi hai.

➡️SAWAL[4]:
Puri surat padhna aur Ayat-e-Sajdah chhod dena kaisa hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Puri surat padhna aur Ayat-e-Sajdah chhod dena  makrooh e 
tahreemi hai ........

➡️SAWAL[5]: 
Agar koi shakhs apni namaz padhte hue Ayat-e-Sajdah sun le
to kya hukm hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Namaz mein nahin, us ne Ayat-e-Sajdah parhi aur namazi ne 
sun liya to namaz ke ba'd Sajdah kare.

➡️SAWAL[6]:
Agar kisi na-baalig bachche se Ayat-e-Sajdah suni to sajda 
karna kya hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Agar kisi na-baalig bachche se Ayat-e-Sajdah suni to sajda 
karna wajib hai ......

➡️SAWAL[7]:
Quran e paak me aayat e sajda kitne hain?
⭐JAWAB:
Quran e paak me aayat e sajda 14 hain....

➡️SAWAL[8]:
Kya Sajdah-e-Tilawat bhi fasid ho jata hai?
⭐JAWAB: 
Haan, jo cheezein namaz ko fasid karti hain, unse Sajdah bhi 
fasid ho jayega.

➡️sawal[9]: 
Kya Aayate Sajdah likhne ya dekhne se Sajdah wajib hota hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Nahin, Aayate Sajdah likhne ya dekhne se Sajdah wajib nahin 
hota.

➡️SAWAL[10]: 
Sajdah-e-Tilawat ka kya tarika hai?
⭐JAWAB: 
Sajdah e tilawat ka karika ye hai
➡Khada ho kar "Allahu Akbar" keh kar Sajde mein jaye, 
aur kam se kam teen baar "Subhan Rabbiyal A'la" kehe,
phir "Allahu Akbar" keh kar khada ho jaye.

➡️SAWAL[11]:
Agar baith kar sajda Kiya tho sajda Ada ho ga ya nahi ?
A) haa Ada ho gaya 
B) nahi Ada hua
C) llm hasil karenge
⭐JAWAB: 
Ada Ho Jayega Magar Masnoon Yahi Hai Ki Khada Ho Kar 
Sajda Mein Jaye Aur Sajda Ke Baad Phir Khada Ho.

➡️SAWAL[12]: 
Ek Majlis Mein Sajda Ki Ek Aayat Ko Kai Baar Padha To Ek 
Sajda Wajib Hoga Ya Kai Sajda?
⭐JAWAB:
Ek Majlis Mein Sajda Ki Ek Aayat Ko Kai Baar Baar Padhne 
Ya Sunne Se Ek Hi Sajda Wajib Hota Hai.

➡️SAWAL[13]: 
Majlis Mein Aayat Padhi Ya Suni Aur Sajda Kar Liya Phir 
Usi Majlis Mein Vahi Aayat Padhi Ya Suni To Doosra Sajda 
Wajib Hoga Ya Nahi?
⭐JAWAB:
Majlis Mein Aayat Padhi Ya Suni Aur Sajda Kar Liya Phir 
Usi Majlis Mein Vahi Aayat Padhi Ya Suni To Doosra Sajda 
Nahi Wajib Hoga Vahi Pahla Sajda Kaafi Hai.

➡️Sawal[14]:
Quran phadte waqt aayat e sajda aaaya tu sajda kon karna 
chahiye? 
⭐Jawab:
Quran phad te waqt aayat e sajda aaya tu quran phad ne 
aur sune wale donu bhi sajda karna lazim hai 

➡️Sawal[15]:
Sajad e tilawat kis kahete hai? 
⭐Jawab:
Quran me 14 muqamat aise hai ke jin ke phad ne ya sun 
ne se sajda wajib hota hai ise sajda  tilawat kahete hai

➡️Sawal[16]:
Sajda e tilawat ka tariqa kya hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Sajad e tilawat ka masnun tariqa ye hai khade hokar 
allhahu akbar kahete howe sajde me jaye aur kam se 
kam teen martaba subhana rabi al ala kahe phir allhahu 
akbar kahete howe khade ho jaye bad  Na is me allhahu 
akbar kahete howe haat uthana hai aur na is me 
tashahud hai aur na salam

➡️Sawal[17]:
Quran ki tilawat me aayat e sajda aaye tu khada ho 
kar phir sajda karna zarori hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Aayat e sajda aaye tu baith kar bhi sajda karsakte hain 
Lekin sajde ke aadab ye hai ke khade ho kat allahu akbar 
kahete howe sajde me jaye
