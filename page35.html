ho tu bhi qurbani wajib hoti hai

➡️SAWAL[9]:
Qurbani wajib hone ke liye kitne sharit hai aur wo kon kon 
se hai?
⭐JAWAB:
Qurbani wajib hone ke liye  4 sharit hain aur wo 
1) Islam
2) iqaamat 
3) tawangari
4) hurriyat
1)ISLAM:- Yani gair muslim pe Qurbaani wajjb nahin.
2)IQAAMAT:- Yani muqeem hona musaafir par wajib nahin.
3)TAWANGARI:- Yani maalik nisaab hona yahan maaldaari se 
murad wahi hai jis se sadqa e fitr wajib hota hai oh murad 
nahin jis se zakat wajib  hoti hai. Nisab ki tafseel nisab 
ke bayan men mulahaza farmaye
4). HURRIYYAT:- Yani aazad hona jo aazad na ho us pe Qurbaani 
wajib nahin keh gulam ke paas  maal hi nahin lihaza ibadat e 
maaliya us pe wajib nahin
