✨ SHABAN UL MOAZZAM ✨ 

   
➡️Sawal[1]:
Shaban ul muazam ki mahine ka Chand dekna kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Shaban ul muazam ki mahine ka Chand dekna wajib e 
kifaya hai

➡️Sawal[2]:
Shaban ul muazam ki mahine me (14 aur 15 ) ke din 
roza rahna kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Shaban ul muazam ki mahine me (14 aur 15 ) ke din 
roza rahna nahfil hai....agar Roza rahna hai tho 
doo roze rahna chahiye.....

➡️Sawal[3]:
Shaban ul muazam ki 15 vi shab ko kya kahete hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Shaban ul muazam ki 15 vi shab ko shab e barat kahete 
hai 

➡️Sawal[4]:
AasmaanKay farishthon Kay liye kon se raat eid aur 
khushi ki hayn?
⭐Jawab:
Asmaan Kay farishthon Kay liye shab e qadar ki raat 
aur shab e baraat ki raat eid aur khushi ki hai.....
jise duniya me musalman ku liye doo eid ke din hai.....
