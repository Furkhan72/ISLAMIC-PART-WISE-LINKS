✨NAMAZ ME SADJA E SAHU KE MASAIL ✨ 


➡️Sawal[1]:
Sure fatiha ke baad agar surat milana 
bhol gaye  aur ruku me yaad  aaya tu kya karen?
⭐Jawab:
agar surat milana bhol jaye aur ruku me yaad aaye 
tu khade ho jaye aur surat milaye phir ruku kare 
aur akhir me sajda sahu karen 

➡️Sawal[2]:
Sajda sahu kis kahete hai
⭐Jawab:
Sahu ke maane bhol ne ke hai kabi namaz me bhol se 
kohi khas kharabi payida hoja ti hai is kharabi ko 
dur karne ke liye qayida khayira me do sajde ki ye 
jate hai in ko sajda sahu kahte hai

➡️Sawal[3]:
Kya namaz ki paheli rakat me surhe fatiha 2baar phadne 
se sajda sahu wajib hota hai 
⭐Jawab:
Namaz ki paheli rakat me surhe fatiha 2baar phad ne se 
sajda sahu wajib ho jaye ga

➡️Sawal[4]:
Namaaz me sajda e sahu kab kar na chahi ye?
⭐Jawab:
Namaaz me kisi wajib ke chot ne per sajda e sahu karna 
chahiye...
