✨IMAMAT KE MASAIL ✨ 

➡️SAWAL[1]:
Imaamat ka sabse ziyaada haqdaar kaun hai ?
⭐JAWAB:
➡Imaamat ka sabse ziyaada haqdaar woh shaks hai 
jo namaaz wa tahaarath ke ahkaam sab se ziyaada 
jaanta ho. phir woh shaks jo tajweed yaani qiraat ki 
jaankaari ziyaada rakhta ho, agar koyi shaks inn baato 
me baraabar ho toh woh shaks ziyaada haqdaar hai jo 
ziyaada muttaqi ho agar iss me bhi baraabar ho phir 
ziyaada tahajjud guzaar. Garz ye ki chand aadmi baraabar 
ho toh unn me jo sharee tarjeeh rakhta ho wahi ziyaada 
haqdaar hai.

➡️Sawal[2]:
Jamat farz hai ya wajib?
⭐Jawab:
Jamat wajib hai jamat ke saat namaz phadne se sattais 
namazon ka sawab milta hai

➡️Sawal[3]:
Kin logon ko imam banana gunha hai 
⭐JAWAB:
➡Faasiq molin jaise sharaabi, juaari, zinakaari, soodkhoor, 
chugalkhoor aur daadhi mundaane waala ya kata kar ek musht 
(mutthi) se kam rakh ne waala aur woh badd mazhab ki jis ki 
badd mazhabi hadde kufr ko na pahunchi ho, unn loogo ko imaam 
banaana gunah hai aur unke peeche namaaz makruh tehreemi 
waajibul iaada hai.

➡️Sawal[4]:
Imam sahab ko agar sajde me paya tu kya jamat me shamil 
ho sakte hai ?
⭐Jawab:
Agar imam sahab sajde ho tu fauran shamil ho jaye ta ke 
rahemat e ilahi ka ziyada se ziyada haq dar ho Magar us 
ki wo rakat mani nahi jaye gi 

➡️Sawal[5]:
Na balig bache ki qirat durust ho tu imam bana sakte hai 
⭐Jawab:
Na balig bache ki qirat durust ho tu imam nahi bana sakte
kyon ki imamat ke liye balig hona shart hai

⭐JAWAB:

bara bar yani [imam ke pao (leg) ke aage nahi hona chahiye]
right  side me kada hona chahiye............

➡️Sawal[9]:
imaam jab qirat kare buland aawaz se khwaah aahista uss waqt

⭐Jawab:
Imaam jab qirat kare buland aawaz se khwaah aashista uss waqt

➡️Sawal[10]:
Imam se pahele muqtadi ka ruku wo sojood wagairah me jaana ya 
usse pahele sar uthaan aisa kar na kya hai
⭐Jawab:
Imaam se pehle muqtadi ka ruku wa sujood wagairah me jaana ya 
usse pehle sar uthaana yeh baate makruh tehreemi hai.

➡️Sawal[11]:
Imam rukuh sajde me der karahe  hai tu muqtadi kya kere
⭐Jawab:
Imam rukuh sajde me der karahe hai tu muqtadi tasbhi ko 
dhurae tab tak dhurae imam sahab rukhu se qhume ki taraf 
aur sajde se qaede ki taraf na aaye
