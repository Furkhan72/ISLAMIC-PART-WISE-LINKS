✨NAMAAZ ME [RUKU] KE MASAIL ✨ 

➡️Sawal[1]:
Qauma y’ani ruku’ se seedha khada hona, aur kam se kam 
ek bar Sub’han ALLAH kehne ki miqdaar theharna kya hai ? 
⭐Jawab:
Qauma y’ani ruku’ se seedha khada hona, aur kam se kam ek 
bar Sub’han ALLAH kehne ki miqdaar theharna wajib hai ........

➡️Sawal[2]:
Qira’at ke b’ad foran ruku’ karna Kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Qira’at ke b’ad foran ruku’ karna wajib hai....

➡️Sawal[3]:
T’adeel e arkaan y’ani har rukn ko itminaan se ada karna y’ani 
ruku’, sajda, qauma aur jalse mein ek bar Sub’han ALLAH kehne 
ki miqdaar theharna kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
T’adeel e arkaan y’ani har rukn ko itminaan se ada karna y’ani 
ruku’, sajda, qauma aur jalse mein ek bar Sub’han ALLAH kehne 
ki miqdaar theharna wajib hai....

➡️Sawal[4]:
Ruku’ mein mard ka sar ko peeth ke barabar rakhna na 
bilkul jhukaye na uncha karke rakhe.  Aisa sajda karna 
kya hai?
⭐Jawab:
Ruku’ mein mard ka sar ko peeth ke barabar rakhna na 
bilkul jhukaye na uncha karke rakhe.  Aisa sajda 
karna sunnat hai

➡️Sawal[5]:

⭐Jawab:
Tanha farz namaz padhne wale ko “sami Allahu liman hamidah” aur 
“Rabbana lakal hamd” donon kehna sunnat hai.....

➡️Sawal[6]:

⭐Jawab:
